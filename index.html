Clagary North and West Stake Youth Conference

June 25, 26 & 27, 2021

Last updated 1:30 PM June 23, 2021

Calgary North Stake and Calgary West Stake youth conference Friday June 25 through Sunday June 26, 2021.

All youth between the ages of twelve and eightteen are invited to participate (including all youth who turn twelve in 2021). Registration is required. Youth may participate either in a combination of virtually and in person, or only virtually (see schedule for details).

Youth conference registration is still open. To register, please send an email including your name, age and ward to calgarynorthstakeyouth@gmail.com.

Please Read

The infomation on this site is being actively updated throughout the day on Wednesday, June 23rd and will continue to be updated with additional information on Thursday June 24 and Friday June 25. Please check back frequently.
