2021 Calgary North and West Stake Youth Festival

Festival is planned in compliance with Alberta Stage 3 COVID-19 restrictions

Youth Dance

The Youth Conference dance will be held at the Reunion Chapel in Airdrie from 8:30PM-11:30PM on Saturday, July 3, 2020.

Due to weather and logistical concerns, the dance had been relocatied to the Reunion Chapel in Airdire and will be held indoors.

Please Note

  • Dance is for youth 14 years old and older
  • Guests accompanied by youth of the North or West stake are welcome
  • All youth, including guests, will require a dance card
  • Dress and conduct standards will be in effect
