Calgary North and West Stake Youth Festival - A Great Work

June 25, 26 & 27, 2021

Last updated 4:00 PM June 24, 2021

"Wherefore, be not weary in well-doing, for ye are laying the foundation of a great work. And out of small things proceedeth that which is great. Behold, the Lord requireth the heart and a willing mind;"

Doctrine & Covenants 64:33-34

All youth and guests born in 2009 or earlier are invited to participate. Participation my be in a combination of virtually and in person, or only virtually. See schedule for details.

Registration is required. Youth festival registration is still open. To register, please send an email including your name, age and ward to calgarynorthstakeyouth@gmail.com.

Building Assignments

Youth who participate in person will attend Friday and Saturday activities at one of the following buildings (based on year of birth):

Youth Ages 14-18

  • All youth born in 2003, 2004, 2005 and 2006 will attend at the North Stake Center (5927 Tipton Road NW, Calgary Alberta)
  • Youth turning 15 in 2021 should attend here

Youth Ages 11-14

  • All youth born in 2007, 2008 and 2009 will attend at the West Stake Center (6560 Silver Spring Crescent NW, Calgary)
  • Youth turning 12 in 2021 should attend here.

Youth will attend the Sunday testimony meeting at thier home stake center regardless of building assignment for Friday and Saturday.
