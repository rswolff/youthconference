Calgary North and West Stake Youth Conference

June 25, 26 & 27, 2021

Last updated 11:00 AM June 24, 2021

Calgary North Stake and Calgary West Stake Youth Conference Festival Friday June 25 through Sunday June 27, 2021.

All youth between the ages of twelve and eighteen are invited to participate (including all youth who turn twelve in 2021). Registration is required. Youth may participate either in a combination of virtually and in person, or only virtually (see schedule for details).

Youth conference registration is still open. To register, please send an email including your name, age and ward to calgarynorthstakeyouth@gmail.com.

Please Read

The infomation on this site is being updated throughout the day on Wednesday June 23rd and Thursday June 24. Please check back frequently.

Building Assignments

Youth who participate in person will attend Friday and Saturday activities at one of the following buildings (based on year of birth):

Youth Ages 14-18

  • All youth born in 2003, 2004, 2005, 2006
  • Attend at the North Stake Center (5927 Tipton Road NW, Calgary Alberta)
  • Youth turning 15 in 2021 should attend here

Youth Ages 11-14

  • All youth born in 2007, 2008, 2009
  • Attend at the West Stake Center (6560 Silver Spring Crescent NW, Calgary)
  • Youth turning 12 in 2021 should attend here.

Note: Youth will attend the Sunday testimony meeting at thier home stake center.
