Clagary North and West Stake Youth Conference

June 25, 26 & 27, 2021

Last updated 6:00AM June 23, 2021

The Calgary North Stake and Calgary West Stake will host a multi-stake youth conference on Friday June 25 through Sunday June 26, 2021.

All youth between the ages of twelve and eightteen are invited to participate (including all youth who turn twelve in 2021). Registration is required. Youth may participate either in a combination of virtually and in person, or only virtually (see schedule for details).

Youth conference registration is still open. Please send an email to: calgarynorthstakeyouth@gmail.com to register.
